Stress Level Assessment

Answer the following questions honestly to check your current stress level.

1. How often do you feel stressed?

2. Do you find it hard to concentrate when stressed?

3. How often do you feel anxious or restless?

4. How often do you feel overwhelmed?

5. How often do you feel irritable or frustrated?

6. How often do you feel physically tense or fatigued?

7. How often do you feel your stress affects your sleep?

8. How often do you feel anxious about future events?

9. How often do you feel your workload is too much?

10. How often do you feel your stress impacts your mood?
