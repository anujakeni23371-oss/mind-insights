Welcome to Mind Insights. This tool helps you understand your stress level and reflect on your mental wellbeing through a short self-assessment.

Stress can affect sleep, concentration, mood, and physical health. Taking a moment to reflect on how you feel can help you recognise when you may need rest, support, or healthier coping strategies.
