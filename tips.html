Stress Management Tips

Based on your stress score, here are some helpful tips to manage and reduce stress:

1. Deep Breathing

Take slow, deep breaths to calm your nervous system and reduce tension.

2. Physical Activity

Exercise regularly to release endorphins and improve mood.

3. Healthy Sleep

Maintain a consistent sleep schedule to help your body recover and reduce stress.

4. Mindfulness & Meditation

Practice mindfulness or meditation daily to stay centered and relaxed.

5. Time Management

Break tasks into smaller steps, prioritize, and take breaks to avoid feeling overwhelmed.

This is a self-assessment guide for stress awareness. It is not a medical or psychological diagnosis.
