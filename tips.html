Stress Management Tips

Low Stress Tips

Maintain your healthy routines and keep track of your workload.

Practice mindfulness and relaxation exercises regularly.

Moderate Stress Tips

Take short breaks during study or work sessions.

Talk to friends or family about how you feel.

Try light physical activity or breathing exercises.

High Stress Tips

Consider professional guidance or counseling.

Engage in regular exercise and structured stress management techniques.

Plan your day and prioritize tasks to reduce overload.
